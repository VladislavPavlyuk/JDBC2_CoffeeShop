Gulab Jamun - Deep-fried dough balls soaked in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose
Rasgulla - Soft, spongy cheese balls soaked in light sugar syrup.
Jalebi - Crispy, spiral-shaped fried batter soaked in saffron-flavored sugar syrup.
Kaju Katli - Diamond-shaped cashew fudge often garnished with edible silver foil.
Ladoo - Round sweets made from various ingredients like gram flour, semolina, or coconut.
Halwa - A dense, sweet pudding made from ingredients like semolina, lentils, or carrots.
Peda - Soft, milk-based sweets often flavored with cardamom and saffron.
Soan Papdi - Flaky, cube-shaped sweet made from gram flour, sugar, and ghee.
Mysore Pak - Rich, buttery sweet made from gram flour, sugar, and ghee.
Phirni - Creamy rice pudding flavored with cardamom and saffron, often garnished with nuts.
