Singh - Derived from the Sanskrit word “Simha,” meaning “lion.” Common among Sikhs and North Indians.
Kumar - Means “child,” “son,” or “prince” in Sanskrit.
Sharma - A common Brahmin surname, meaning “protection” or "refuge".
Patel - A Gujarati surname meaning “village headman” or "landowner".
Devi - Means “goddess” in Sanskrit, often used by women in various regions.
Das - Means “servant” or “devotee” in Sanskrit.
Kaur - Means “princess,” commonly used by Sikh women.
Yadav - Refers to descendants of Yadu, a legendary king.
Gupta - Means “protector” or “governor” in Sanskrit.
Chowdhury - A hereditary title meaning “holder of four,” often used in Bengal.
